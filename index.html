About VeriSOC

VeriSOC is an initiative designed to ensure that users on social media platforms are real and trustworthy. By verifying identities through authenticated KYC methods like Aadhaar and Passport validation, VeriSOC combats the rising threats of misinformation, cyber fraud, and online impersonation.

Our goal is to strengthen the Indian digital ecosystem by creating a secure, transparent, and reliable social media environment for every citizen.

Key Features

  • 🔐 Aadhaar / Passport-based KYC Authentication
  • ⚠ AI-powered Misinformation Detection
  • 🛡 Controlled Access for Anonymous Users
  • 📊 Admin Dashboard to Track and Flag Content
  • 🚀 Fast, Secure, and User-friendly Verification Process
